As the year is drawing to a close, a lot of people have been posting generated images featuring selected examples of their 2010 Facebook updates. I tried doing this, but the updates that were selected turned out to be incredibly boring, mostly involving complaints about London Underground.

Luckily, I discovered a thing called PummelVision, a funky little tool that will take all your photos from a web account (Facebook, Flickr, etc) and compile them into a short movie. All I had to do was authenticate my Facebook and YouTube accounts and a few hours later - during which I went out for a pint - an e-mail appeared telling me my video was ready.

And here it is, the last few years of my life, condensed in to 3 minutes of still frames.

Christ, I've been to a lot of weddings.

Why not give it a go? You might be surprised to see how much fun you've been having lately!
