Moved from London to New York. Thought, heck it's a big city, how different could life be?

  1. Cat people - people who love cats, and people who ARE cats
  2. The subway is awesome, and terrible at the same time. The tube was mildly annoying. The subway is convenient, but EVIL
  3. People are angry (the van thing)
  4. People are noisy
  5. You need winter clothes and summer clothes (never owned so many shorts, nor boots for snow)
