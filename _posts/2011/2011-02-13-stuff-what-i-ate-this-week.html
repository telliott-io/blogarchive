It's been a while since I posted here. I won't make any excuses, but here's a slideshow of everything I ate last week.

Intrigued by the idea of self experimentation in healthy living, I've been reading The 4-Hour Body by Tim Ferriss. I've only really read the diet parts so far, but it's been a fascinating read, with an emphasis on constant monitoring and measuring to find an approach that really works for you.

In the hope of having what Ferriss calls a "Harajuku moment" (from a charming story about badly fitting designer clothes), I've took on board the idea of photographing everything I eat and drink to shock me into making healthier choices.

In a true quantum effect, this isn't really representative of what I was eating during a typical week. The idea of everyone seeing what I was eating discouraged random biscuit snacking. But heck, that's a start.
