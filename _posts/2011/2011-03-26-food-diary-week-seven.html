Another week and another hundred-odd pictures of food. Making some marginally better choices this week, but still room for improvement.

Water's slowly moving up the ranking, but still clear second to tea. In fact, tea increased by a full 8 cups! Alcohol, on the other hand was way down, probably just a result of not having a series of boozy work dos, but looks good in the stats.

Cutting out cheese seems to have done me a power of good as well. I'm doing better at the gym, feeling less sluggish during the day and even lost a few pounds. Maybe the weight loss is more down to my lunch choices being more limited (hence more bagels and less heavy full-plate lunches), but can't think of alternative reasons for the other effects - ok, I'm guessing, I've always been too lazy to employ rigorous experimental methods.

Plan for next week? More water, less tea and keeping the booze under control.
