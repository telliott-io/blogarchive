In case the nonsensical title didn't make it clear, I've not blogged in a while. For some odd reason, I've been struggling to think of something interesting to write about. Not that there isn't enough in the news.

Or I could express my fears about the implications of a tragic polar bear attack and it's inevitable hijacking by climate change skeptics.

Instead I'm just going to post a picture of a laser cat.
