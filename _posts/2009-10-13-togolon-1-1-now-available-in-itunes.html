Well, its official, Togolon 1.1 is out the door and approved! The new version adds support for Scoreloop, providing an online leaderboard and allows you to issue and respond to challenges from players the world over. And still for the low price of £0.59 ($0.99)!

Scoreloop gives Togolon players access to an online community of mobile gamers, where you can:

  • Compare scores
  • Challenge players to races on single levels or whole episodes
  • Publish scores and big wins on Facebook
  • Make new friends through Buddy Lists

I'd also like to offer a huge thanks to the beta testers who volunteered to try out this new version of Togolon before it was submitted.
